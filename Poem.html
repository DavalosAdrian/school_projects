Two Dead Boys


Ladies and gentlemen
I stand before you showing that I stand behind you,
To tell you something that I know absolutely nothing about.
Admission is free please pay at the door,
Pull up a chair and sit on the floor.
I'll recite a rhyme by saying nothing at all.......

One bright day in the middle of the night,
Two dead boys got up to fight,
Back to back they faced each other,
Drew their swords and shot each other,

One was blind and the other couldn't, see
So they chose a statue for a referee.
A blind man went to see them play,
A mute man went to shout "hooray!"

A legless donkey trotting by,
Kicked the blind man in the eye.
Knocked him through a nine inch wall,
Into a dry ditch and drowned them all.

A deaf policeman heard the noise,
And came to arrest the two dead boys,
If you don't believe this lie is true,
Ask the blind man he saw it too!
